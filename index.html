Project Murakami 2025

Project Murakami was a year long expansive collaboration project with CASETiFY, that spanned across 5 activations and major events throughout the year, sprawling between US, Hong Kong, Japan, Korea. As well as global retail takeovers. It was an ambitious and massive endeavor that reached phenomal coverage, sales and helped elevate CASETiFY to a new level of sophistication.

Evangelion Shinjuku

To celebrate the release of its highly-anticipated Evangelion “PROJECT-CSTF: PROTECTION from IMPACT” collaboration, global tech accessory brand CASETiFY recently put together the “IMPACT!” launch event. Ahead of the drop of the special range, CASETiFY and Japanese animation studio ©Khara unveiled a four-meter high, seven-meter-wide EVA-01 in the heart of Tokyo’s Shinjuku district. The impressive display also shows the iconic mecha holding a CASETiFY Impact Case featuring a design from the collaboration.
